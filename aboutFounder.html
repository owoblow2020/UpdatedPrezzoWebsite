Emma Njoku is a consummate and dedicated executive with an exceptional entrepreneurial mindset.

With sterling academic records and professional background, he continues to tower above his contemporaries, with businesses spanning across various continents.

  • A Path-finder.

He is the MD of Prezzo Shed Investments Limited, a multinational company supporting the Nigerian and African healthcare sectors. He also renders services in the real estate sector and currently sits on the board of many companies in Nigeria and overseas.
